Things I'd Love
to Find This Season

A careful list — things worth seeking out. Consider this less a registry and more a set of trail markers. Follow any one of them, or none at all.

CuratorEugenie Gruman
SeasonSpring / Summer
Items7 listed
Experience
A museum membership for the year
MoMA, the Met, the Whitney — any one-year pass. I'd use it constantly, on quiet mornings and rainy afternoons. The kind of gift that keeps arriving.
$60 – 120
Reading
Books you'd personally recommend
Fiction, essays, nature writing, memoir — whatever moved you recently. A book chosen by someone I love always turns out to be the right one.
~$20
Morning Ritual
Specialty coffee subscription — 3 months
Single-origin, interesting provenance, tasting notes worth reading. There's something about a considered cup that makes the whole morning feel earned.
$45 – 75
Home
A candle — warm, woodsy, or earthy
Diptyque, Aesop, or a smaller maker you trust. Cedar, vetiver, fig. Something to light when the light outside is just right.
$30 – 60
Skin
A fine hand cream
The kind I'd never justify for myself — something with a considered scent and weight to it. L'Occitane, Aesop, whatever you'd choose.
~$25
From the Heart
Something only you could give
Handmade, foraged, handpicked, or simply something you felt I should have. These are the gifts I remember longest.
