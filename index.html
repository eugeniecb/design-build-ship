Wishlist App

A design exploration — one wishlist, many visual voices. Watch the style evolve.

v01

v01 — Warm & Friendly

The starting point: soft cream tones, Georgia serif, emoji icon tiles, and a single centered column. Personal and approachable.
v02

v02 — Magazine Editorial

A sharp turn: high contrast, oxblood accent, and a 12-column asymmetric grid with a hero item. Typographic and restrained.
v03

v03 — Brutalist

Raw and confrontational: black borders, yellow warning bar, monospace type, and items as a stark numbered ledger.
v04

v04 — Luxury Boutique

Near-black background, warm gold accents, Roman numerals, and restrained serif prose. Quiet and expensive.
v05 ✨

v05 — Gen Z

Purple and pink, Space Grotesk, pastel emoji tiles, and lowercase energy. Playful, colorful, very online.
v06

v06 — Outdoorsy & Earthy

Deep forest green, warm parchment, and a vertical trail of dot-and-line markers. Nature-inspired without being tacky.
v07 ✂

v07 — Scrapbook Collage

Kraft paper background, rotated cards with tape strips, cut-out title blocks, and Caveat handwritten font throughout.
~*~ v08 ~*~

v08 — Retro 90s Web

Marquee text, rainbow title, Comic Sans, a visitor counter, 88×31 buttons, and a table-based layout. Geocities forever.
✦ v09 ✦

v09 — Y2K

Chrome gradient title, starfield background, iridescent CD-disc icons, glowing neon tags, and a glass-morphism card layout.
v10

v10 — E-commerce

Clean white e-commerce feel: pill filters, photo-card grid, progress bars, "Contribute" buttons, and a top nav. Inspired by Zola.
v11 ✨

v11 — Gen Z × E-commerce

v05's purple/pink palette and Space Grotesk energy merged with v10's full registry layout: nav, filters, card grid, progress bars, and CTA buttons.
v12

v12 — Square Product Cards

Flush tile grid, muted earth swatches, and a compact info strip. Clean and minimal with DM Sans — no clutter.
v13 ✨

v13 — Gen Z Square Grid

v11's purple/pink palette and e-commerce layout, simplified: square cards, pastel swatches, compact info strip, no clutter.
v14

v14 — Warm Square Grid

v13's square card layout with v01's warm palette: terracotta accents, peach swatches, and a soft cream background.
v15

v15 — Serif Square Grid

v14's warm square layout with v06's Georgia serif typography: italic card names, light-weight headings, and a serif/sans pairing throughout.
v16

v16 — Post-it Hero

v15 with the title and welcome message on a yellow post-it note, taped to a dark background — a nod to v07's scrapbook collage.
v17 ✦

v17 — Gender-Neutral Gen Z

v16's serif square layout with a fresh palette: electric lime, bright orange, and near-black. Playful and bold without leaning pink or purple.
v18

v18 — Earth Tones Square Grid
